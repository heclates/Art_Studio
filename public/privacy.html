Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Aleksandrova Art Studio ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our website aleksandrova-art-studio.cz and our reservation system.

1. Information We Collect

When you make a reservation or use our services, we may collect:

  • Name and contact information (email address and phone number).
  • Reservation details (date, time, type of art class).
  • Google Account information (if you use Google Sign-In).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Process and manage your art class reservations.
  • Send confirmation emails and updates regarding your booking.
  • Sync reservation data with our Google Sheets for internal scheduling.

3. Data Sharing and Google API Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We use Google OAuth and Google Sheets API to store and manage your reservation data. Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy.

4. Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
